# Demo Video Checklist

Aim for a true gameplay demo of 60 to 90 seconds.

1. Open on the title screen for three seconds.
2. Say: "Daybreak Protocol turns the June solstice into a code-breaking clock."
3. Start the game and point out the moving sun, daylight timer, and Bombe relays.
4. Deliberately choose one wrong answer to show that contradictions consume daylight.
5. Solve the XOR or Bombe puzzle and show the educational explanation.
6. Cut to the final transmission, solve it, and show one of the endings.
7. Close with: "Built from scratch in JavaFX for the June Solstice Game Jam, as an ode to Alan Turing."

Record the actual game window. Do not use a slideshow in place of gameplay.
